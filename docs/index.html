Photoflare

Your Photos. Your Server. Your Privacy.

The premier native iOS client for PhotoPrism. Access your self-hosted photo library from anywhere with a beautifully designed, privacy-focused app.

Download on App Store Learn More

Browse & Discover

Explore your entire photo library in a stunning, responsive grid with smooth scrolling through thousands of images.

  • Full media support — photos, videos, and Live Photos with native playback
  • Smooth zoom — pinch to view full-resolution details
  • Photo metadata — camera info, lens data, location, and more
  • Intelligent caching — thumbnails load instantly, even on slow connections

Organize Your Way

Create, edit, and manage albums directly from your device. Everything you need to keep your library tidy.

  • Album management — create, rename, and organize collections on the go
  • Favourites — mark your best shots for quick access
  • Bulk actions — multi-select photos to delete or add to albums
  • Map browsing — explore photos on an interactive map by location

Privacy First

Your photos stay on your server. Photoflare never routes data through third-party services.

  • Private items — hide sensitive photos from your main library
  • 2FA & OIDC — two-factor authentication and OpenID Connect support
  • SSL/TLS — all connections are encrypted end-to-end
  • No third-party servers — your data never leaves your infrastructure

Stay In Sync

Your library is always up to date — across devices and in the background.

  • WebSocket real-time — new photos appear instantly as they're added to your server
  • Background photo upload — automatically sync photos from your device
  • iCloud events — keep all your devices in sync with small, anonymous events. Learn more
