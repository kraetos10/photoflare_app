Powerful Features

Everything you need to access your PhotoPrism library on the go

📷

Browse Your Entire Library

Explore your photos and videos in a stunning, responsive grid that adapts to any screen size. Enjoy smooth scrolling through thousands of images with intelligent thumbnail caching.

🎬

Full Media Support

View all your media types with native support:

  • Photos in full resolution with smooth zoom
  • Videos with built-in player and native controls
  • Live Photos with full playback support
📁

Album Management

Create, edit, and organize albums directly from your device. Browse existing collections, add photos to albums.

⬆️

Easy Uploads

Upload photos and videos directly to your PhotoPrism server. Select from your photo library or import files, with real-time progress tracking and automatic album assignment.

🔒

Secure Authentication

Multiple secure authentication options:

  • Username & password login
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA/OTP)
  • OpenID Connect (OIDC) support
  • Secure SSL/TLS connections
📴

Offline Mode

Download your photos for offline access. View your library anywhere, even without an internet connection. Smart caching keeps your favorites ready when you need them.

🔄

Seamless Sync

Real-time synchronization keeps your library up-to-date via WebSocket connection. See new photos appear instantly as they're added to your server.

🔒

Private Photos

Keep your sensitive photos secure with Private Photos support. Private items are hidden from your main library and protected from prying eyes.

🔄

Photo Sync

Automatically sync photos from your device to your PhotoPrism server in the background. Your library stays up to date without lifting a finger. Coming in v1.5.

🎨

Beautiful Design

A native experience built with care:

  • Full Dark Mode support
  • Haptic feedback for tactile interactions

Your Privacy, Guaranteed

Photoflare connects directly to YOUR PhotoPrism server. Your photos never pass through our servers. Your data stays yours, always.
