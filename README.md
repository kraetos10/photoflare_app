# Photoflare

A native iOS client for [PhotoPrism](https://photoprism.app) - your self-hosted photo management solution.

## Features

- **Browse Your Library** - View and explore your entire photo collection with a beautiful, native iOS interface
- **Album Management** - Create, edit, and organize albums directly from your device
- **Photo Uploads** - Upload photos from your camera roll or Files app to your PhotoPrism server
- **Offline Mode** - Access your synced photos even without an internet connection
- **Fast Sync** - Efficiently sync your library with background refresh support
- **Native Experience** - Built with SwiftUI for a smooth, responsive experience that feels right at home on iOS

## Getting Started

1. Download Photoflare from the App Store or TestFlight
2. Enter your PhotoPrism server address
3. Log in with your credentials
4. Start browsing your photo library

## Reporting Bugs

Please use the [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/nicktsioros/photoflare_app/issues) to report any bugs or issues you encounter.

When reporting a bug, please include:
- Device model and iOS version
- App version

- Any error messages you see

## Troubleshooting

### Beta: App crashes on launch

If you are using the Beta version and the app crashes when opening, please delete the app and re-install it from TestFlight.

## Requirements

- iOS 26.0 or later
- A PhotoPrism server instance
